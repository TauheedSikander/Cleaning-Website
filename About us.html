About us

Committed to Keeping
Your Home Squeaky Clean

16 years ago we once heard a sentence “Big
enough to do the job, small enough to care”,
and the philosophy captured in that one
sentence stuck with us.

Since we began our company, one of our
goals was to grow while keeping the personal
service we provide our customers.
We grow one client at a time with a close eye
on our service quality.
We are a part of many of our clients lives as
we clean homes.
We are there with the pets, the kids, you
name it.

That personal connection and open
communication are what has built us long-
lasting relationships with so many clients.
